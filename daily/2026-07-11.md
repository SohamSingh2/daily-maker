# Capture Micro Journal

Date: 2026-07-11

Tiny app concept: a clean studio **micro journal**.

Core touches:

- export as plain text
- copy-to-clipboard
- one-click reset

Palette:

- Background: `#0b132b`
- Accent: `#5bc0be`
- Text: `#f4f1de`
