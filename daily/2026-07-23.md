# Nudge Recipe Picker

Date: 2026-07-23

Tiny app concept: a warm command center **recipe picker**.

Core touches:

- editable cards
- progress rings
- daily streak tracking

Palette:

- Background: `#101820`
- Accent: `#f2aa4c`
- Text: `#f7f7f2`
